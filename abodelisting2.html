About AbodeListing

AbodeListing is a Rokk3r Labs portfolio company where I designed the user experience and user interface as well as the branding. The AbodeListing entrepreneur had the idea of creating a platform where anyone has the chance to buy or sell real estate without the help of a real estate agent. Rokk3r Labs helped the entrepreneur further develop their idea. The results were a platform where Real Estate Agents services are sold "a-la-carte".
