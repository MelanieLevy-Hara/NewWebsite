About Ridekick

I came up with the idea for Ridekick while I worked at Rokk3r Labs, an incubator in Miami. The mission is to increase carpooling incentives by matching lone drivers with people of similar interests and routes to work. I single-handedly designed the logo and branding, and created the landing page and pitch deck for this project. I am currently creating mockups for the mobile application.
